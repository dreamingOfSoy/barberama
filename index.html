About us

Hey, there. We're Baraberama - an independent barber shop located on Rodney Street, in Liverpool, UK. We were founded in 1982 by our head barber Armie Clipper (yes, that's his real name!) and are one of the longest running barber shops in the north-west. We specialise in modern, stylish haircuts that keep you looking trendy and contemporary. Our uniqueness comes from our attitude and our shop. We promote grassroots events, including hosting a regular open-mic night in the shop, and a vintage clothes shop on Sundays, and promote individualism and self-expression. Our job is to make you feel comfortable, not only in our barber chair, but also in yourself, and that is our vow to you.
